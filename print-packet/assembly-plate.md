Current status: V5 build-packet candidate, not build-ready.

## Dimensions

- Governing model: open-open edge-tone/overtone flute.
- Target: A2, 110.00 Hz at 20 C validator baseline.
- First-pass geometry length: 1550.96 mm.
- Bore ID reference: 31.75 mm from SolidWorks/design-table evidence.
- Hole layout: 68%, 73%, and 83% of long chamber length from the foot end.

## Measurement-Required Before Build Release

- Bore stations at foot, mid-body, side-tube/labium zone, and top.
- Flue width and height.
- Sound-hole length and labium edge distance.
- Side air-tube leak/path/junction-angle checks.
- Fundamental and overblown partial tuner captures at known temperature and
  humidity.
