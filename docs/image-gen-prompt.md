# Image Generation Prompt

Concept visualization only. Do not treat generated images as dimensional, tuning, CAD, DXF, or fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photograph of a finished stave-built fujara concept, an extremely tall Slovak overtone flute with a long main wooden bore, a parallel side air tube, three finger holes low on the body, and a visible labium or voicing window near the top where the side tube meets the main pipe. Warm hardwood staves, oil finish, subtle shepherd-flute silhouette, full height shown with a stool or wall mark for scale, clean studio background, soft directional lighting, three-quarter product angle, about a 50mm lens, high detail.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic image of the fujara staged upright in a bright woodshop or quiet folk rehearsal room, leaning safely on a padded stand, side air tube and three-hole layout visible. Include long clamps, bore gauges, and a design table notebook with no readable text nearby. Natural light, realistic proportions, environmental storytelling around validating a very long side-blown overtone flute.

## Detail/Macro Prompt

Close-up macro of the fujara side-tube junction and labium area: parallel air tube feeding into a carefully cut voicing window on the main bore, crisp splitting edge, oiled hardwood, tight joinery, three-hole body fading into the background, soft raking light, photorealistic high detail.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid logos, watermarks, readable text, recorder-like headjoints, six-hole flute layouts, impossible tube connections, miniature proportions, and fake traditional decoration.
